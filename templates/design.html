Design

I use layout and visual design in concert with written copy to meet users where they're at and guide them intuitively to the information they're looking for. These examples come from revamps to the Dashlane support site.

Redesign of Help Center for brand alignment and usability

Problem: Our Help Center needed updating to align with the company's imminent rebrand.

Solution: I worked closely with a designer to implement a complete overhaul of our Help Center styles and functionality, including a refreshed color scheme, better sizing and spacing, improved responsive design for tables, and modern micro-interactions when hovering on links.

Impact: Aligned support site with new company branding, improving usability and customer confidence.

Reorganization of Help Center information architecture

Problem: Our Help Center was originally created by a support team without a design background, and over time the site became cluttered and overloaded with information.

Solution:

  • I created a logical structure and hierarchy for categories, sections, and articles.
  • I consolidated the number of categories and sections.
  • I redesigned category pages for easier navigation and a more attractive presentation.
